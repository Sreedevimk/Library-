Hanging House

Author:John Burley

sumary

Hanging House by author John Burley is a novel that combines elements of mystery and psychological drama. It explores themes of family secrets, trauma, and the impact of past events on the present. The story centers around a family dealing with a tragic and haunting legacy, and the narrative delves into the psychological complexity of its characters. In terms of a short review, the book is often praised for its gripping plot and well-developed characters. The atmospheric writing and intricate storyline keep readers engaged and intrigued. Some find the novel's exploration of dark themes and family dynamics both compelling and thought-provoking. However, opinions on its pacing and resolution may vary among readers
